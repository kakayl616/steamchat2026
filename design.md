# SteamPanel Design System

## Theme
Full Steam-accurate dark UI.

## Colors
- Background primary: #1b2838 (Steam dark blue)
- Background secondary: #2a475e (Steam mid blue)
- Background tertiary: #16202d (darker panels)
- Background card: #1e2d3d
- Accent blue: #66c0f4 (Steam light blue)
- Accent green: #4c6b22 (Steam green badge)
- Accent green text: #a4d007
- Text primary: #c6d4df
- Text secondary: #8f98a0
- Text bright: #ffffff
- Border: #3d5a73
- Red/danger: #c94040
- Yellow/warning: #d4a017

## Typography
- Font: 'Motiva Sans', 'Arial', sans-serif (Steam's actual font) — fallback to system-ui
- Headings: bold, #c6d4df or white
- Body: #8f98a0 to #c6d4df
- Small labels: uppercase tracking-wider #8f98a0

## Layout
- Dark panel cards with subtle inner borders
- Steam-style gradient headers
- Thin separator lines #3d5a73
- Button style: Steam blue gradient bg-[#4779a4] hover:bg-[#5b8db8]

## Components
- Cards: bg-[#16202d] border border-[#3d5a73]
- Badges: bg-[#4c6b22] text-[#a4d007] for status
- Danger badges: bg-[#4b0000] text-[#c94040]
- Input: bg-[#32373e] border-[#3d5a73] text-[#c6d4df] focus:border-[#66c0f4]
- Steam button: bg-gradient-to-b from-[#4779a4] to-[#3a5f85] hover:from-[#5b8db8] hover:to-[#4779a4]
